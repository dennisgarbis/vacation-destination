Intro

Welcome to my vacation destination. On this site you will find an itinerary listing multiple Greek islands, with a table listing daily activities.

The vacation starts and finishes in Athens, and multiple islands to visit in between.

10-Day Tour

The Cyclades are an island group in the Aegean Sea, southwest of mainland Greece. There are numerous islands that are inhabited, with th most popular ones listed below.

Prior to embarking on your vacation, we will spend one day in Athens, before taking a ferry boat out to the island of Mykonos

Itinerary:
Island To-Do
Day 1 Athens Walk around the Acropolis, see many ancient sites
Days 2-3 Mykonos Visit famous beaches, many tourists, party
Days 4-5 Syros Most populated island in the area, visit locals
Day 6 Paros Visit beaches, historical sites
Day 7 Milos Beautiful landscape, picturesque villages
Days 8-9 Santorini Most famous island in Greece, amazing views
Day 10 Athens Quick coffee and lunch before leaving
